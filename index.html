What We Do

Our O&M Services

Balaji Enterprises is a dedicated telecom Operation & Maintenance service provider — keeping passive tower infrastructure live, compliant and SLA-ready through both field execution and app-based digital management.

📲   Digital O&M & App Management
📲

Alarm App Management

Real-time alarm monitoring and closure through operator/PMC alarm apps — site visibility, ticket acknowledgement, on-ground rectification and timely closure within SLA.

🗓️

PM App Management

Digital PM scheduling and execution via PMC preventive-maintenance apps — checklist-based site visits, photo evidence, geo-tagging and app-based reporting for audit-ready compliance.

📊

Site MIS & Reporting

Structured MIS reporting on alarm closure, PM compliance, uptime and site health — giving PMCs full visibility and audit-ready documentation at all times.

✅

Compliance & Quality Audits

Routine quality checks and compliance audits across managed sites — ensuring app entries, site conditions and documentation match operator standards.

🔧   Field O&M Services
🛠️

Preventive Maintenance (PM)

Scheduled PM visits covering passive infra inspection, battery health checks, shelter cleaning, earthing verification and alarm clearance.

🚨

Breakdown & Corrective Maintenance

Rapid field response to site alarms and equipment failures — fault isolation, component repair and site restoration within operator SLA timelines.

📋

Complete Passive O&M Management

Full turnkey passive O&M of telecom tower sites — security, power management, uptime tracking, MIS reporting and end-to-end documentation.

⚡

24x7 Field Response

Round-the-clock mobilisation for critical alarms and breakdowns — keeping every managed site within uptime SLA commitments.

About Us

Who We Are

Balaji Enterprises is a dedicated telecom O&M service provider headquartered in Ambala, Haryana. We work directly under leading telecom Project Management Companies (PMCs) to deliver complete Operation & Maintenance of passive tower infrastructure.

With hands-on field experience across five states in North India, our teams manage everything from app-based alarm closure and PM scheduling to on-ground preventive and corrective maintenance.

  • Currently working under Aerial Telecom Solutions Pvt. Ltd. & S G Encon Pvt. Ltd.
  • Multi-state operations: Haryana, Punjab, Himachal Pradesh, Rajasthan & UP
  • Alarm App Management & PM App Management for managed sites
  • Full passive O&M — preventive maintenance & breakdown response
  • Structured MIS reporting and compliance documentation
  • GST Registered — GSTIN: 06IXSPS1970N1ZC
